Chocolate Pancakes

Description

These chocolate pancakes with chocolate chips are sweet and slightly gooey. _ Serve warm with fresh fruit and a dusting of powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, and vanilla together in a bowl. Stir in melted butter.
  2. Whisk flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt together in a separate bowl. Pour in wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.
  3. Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat. Working in batches, pour 1/4 cup batter into the hot skillet for each pancake. _ Cook until bubbles form and pop in the center of each pancake, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 1 minute.
